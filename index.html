About Us

Floral Wholesale Solutions

Founded in 2024, Molvin Petals is a leading global partner for wholesale Kenyan flowers.

Our vision is to be the leading global supplier of fresh, high-quality flowers, connecting markets worldwide with the beauty of nature. We strive to set new standards in the floral export industry through innovation, sustainability, and a commitment to excellence, creating lasting relationships and delivering unparalleled value to our customers.

Why Choose Us

Premium Quality Flowers

We ensure that every bloom is of the highest quality, handpicked and sourced from trusted growers globally. Our flowers are fresh, vibrant, and of superior quality, providing you with beautiful arrangements that last longer.

Global Reach

We offer international flower delivery, bringing your messages of love and care across borders. Whether you're sending flowers locally or to distant parts of the world, we have a reliable network to ensure your flowers arrive in perfect condition.

Custom Floral Designs

We specialize in custom flower arrangements tailored to your occasion. Whether it’s for a wedding, birthday, or corporate gift, we craft designs that suit your style, preferences, and the message you wish to convey.

Sustainable Practices

We are committed to sustainable sourcing and environmentally-friendly practices. Our flowers are grown with minimal impact on the environment, ensuring that every bouquet you send is not only beautiful but also eco-conscious.

Reliable Customer Service

Our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist you with any inquiries or issues you might have. Whether it’s tracking an order, customizing an arrangement, or providing advice on flower care, we’re always here to help, ensuring you have a smooth and hassle-free experience.

Affordable Pricing with Exceptional Value

We offer competitive pricing for our premium flowers and services, ensuring that you get great value for your money. Our transparent pricing model means no hidden fees, and we provide various shipping options to fit your budget, without compromising on quality or service.

Our Export Process

Step 1: Order Placement

Start your order with our simple and easy-to-use platform. Select your desired products and quantities, and proceed to checkout.

Step 2: Inventory Confirmation

We confirm the availability of the flowers you have selected. Our inventory management ensures no surprises for our customers.

Step 3: Payment Process

Once your order is confirmed, you can securely pay via multiple payment methods. We ensure smooth and safe transactions.

Step 4: Flower Sourcing

We source the freshest flowers directly from farms, ensuring high quality and freshness for every order.

Step 5: Transportation & Shipping

Our flowers are carefully packed and shipped via our trusted global partners, ensuring the fastest and safest delivery options.

Step 6: Custom Documents

We handle all necessary documentation and customs clearance to ensure smooth, hassle-free international shipping.

Step 7: Follow Up

After delivery, we follow up to ensure customer satisfaction and to handle any queries you may have.
